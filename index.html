Applecrates is a dedicated crate creation tool designed to make DJ sets great again. Whether you're a producer, dj or musician, Applecrates helps you reclaim your creative flow by assisting in library creation and directory organization.

Why I Built It

"I was tired of the friction. My hard drives were full of gold, but the collection process was time consuming. I wanted a tool that could mirror my Apple Music playlists locally, quickly and easily. I built Applecrates to be the simple, efficient solution I couldn't find anywhere else."

— Gregory Hodge, Developer
