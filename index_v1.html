Organize. Browse. Inspire.

Applecrates is a dedicated crate creation tool designed to make DJ sets great again. However, it's not just for DJs. Whether you're a producer, dj or musician, Applecrates helps you reclaim your creative flow by assiting in library creation helping you to structure music directories easily.

Why I Built Applecrates

"I was tired of the friction. My hard drives were full of gold, but the collection and organization process was time consuming and brutal. I also wanted a way to mirror my applemusic playlists on my hard drive locally and easily. I couldn't find anything that does this quickly and efficiently. I wanted a tool that was simple to use and didn't feel like complicated to use."

— DevByGreg, The Founder

Light/Dark Mode

There is a light/dark mode that allows you to switch between themes based on your preference or lighting conditions.

Smart Playlist Duplication

Applecrates quickly and intelligently scans the provided applemusic playlist and mirrors it to your local drive.

Lightweight UI

No bloat. No corporate jargon. Just a clean, modern interface that stays out of your way and lets your sounds take center stage.
